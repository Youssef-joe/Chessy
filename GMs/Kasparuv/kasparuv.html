Garry Kasparov Biography

Garry Kasparov

Date of Birth: April 13, 1963

Nationality: Russian

Notable Achievements

  • 1985 - Became the youngest undisputed World Chess Champion
  • 2005 - Retired from professional chess

Garry became the grandmaster in 1980. Garry was 17 years old at that time and later he qualified for the World Chess Champion title match in 1984 by winning the candidates. Garry reached the highest FIDE rating of all time at 2851. This was the highest ELO rating before Magnus broke the record. Garry is no doubt a chess legend in chess history.
